# ArogyaMitra AI – Intelligent Community Health & Wellness Platform

ArogyaMitra AI is an AI-powered healthcare and community wellness platform designed to provide personalized health insights, environmental risk analysis, nutrition recommendations, and predictive health intelligence. The platform combines user health data, real-time environmental conditions, and generative AI to help individuals make informed health decisions while promoting community well-being.

## Key Features

### User Authentication & Health Profiles

* Secure user registration and login
* Personal health profile management
* BMI calculation and health score generation
* Persistent health record storage using SQLite

### Environmental Intelligence

* Live GPS-based location detection
* Real-time weather monitoring
* Air Quality Index (AQI) analysis
* UV exposure and heatwave risk assessment
* Environmental health alerts

### AI Nutrition Assistant

* Personalized diet recommendations powered by Gemini AI
* Climate-adaptive food suggestions
* Hydration recommendations
* Lifestyle and wellness guidance

### Digital Health Twin

* Virtual health model for every user
* Future health risk prediction
* Personalized preventive recommendations
* Wellness trend analysis

### Community Health Dashboard

* Community-level health intelligence
* Air quality and environmental monitoring
* Heatwave and disease risk alerts
* AI-generated public health recommendations

### Data Persistence

* SQLite database integration
* Health profile history tracking
* Nutrition recommendation history
* Environmental analysis records
* Digital health twin reports

## Project Vision

ArogyaMitra AI aims to bridge the gap between personal healthcare and community wellness by combining artificial intelligence, environmental intelligence, and predictive analytics into a single accessible platform. The system empowers users with actionable health insights while helping communities become more resilient to environmental and public health challenges.
